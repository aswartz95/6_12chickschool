Embryology 101

This project will help the students grades K-4 understancd the embryonic stages of development of a chicken. All classes will be sent a Hatching Journal to record visual observations of the growing embryos, as well as information pertaining to the eggs.

In this series we will cover

  • Chicken research
  • Parts and function of the egg
  • Function and importance of the incubator
  • Difference between oviparous and viviparous animals
  • Responsibility necessary for caring for a living thing
